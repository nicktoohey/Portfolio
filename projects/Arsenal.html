Arsenal+ Dashboard

Arsenal+, Mix+, Match+ dashboard to evaluate induvial pitcher arsenals, overall arsenal leaderboards, induvidual pitch mix+ and match+ leaderboards, and an editor to create and adjust arsenals, allowing users to see how mix-and-match metrics change in real time

Arsenal+, Mix+ and Match+

Arsenal+, Mix+ and Match+ are metrics developed by Driveline Baseball to evaluate pitcher arsenals. Using the trajectory of a pitch, Driveline showed how you can calculate the mix+ (breadth) and match+ (depth) of individual pitches and overall arsenals. For example. a Curveball or Sweeper with generally add breadth to an arsenal improving the mix+ score while a cutter or slider with generally add depth to an arsenal improving the match+ score. From the Mix+ and Match+ metrics you can calculate an overall Arsenal+ metric. All metrics have a mean of 100 with a standard deviation of 20.

Arsenal Leaderboard

The leaderboard page allows you to see pitchers in NCAA DI with the highest graded arsenal+, mix+ or match+ scores. You can filter by pitcher, minimum pitches, team, conference and conference tier. You can also sort by arsenal+, mix+ or match+ score, and by pitcher name. All the data in the picture above is filler data for the demo version.

Pitch Leaderboard

The pitch leaderboard allows you to see the top individual pitches by Arsenal+, Mix+ and Match+ scores. You can filter by pitcher, pitch type, minimum pitches, team, conference and conference tier. You can also sort by arsenal+, mix+ or match+ score, and by pitch name. All the data in the picture above is filler data for the demo version.

Arsenal Explorer

The Arsenal Explorer allows you to search by pitcher and see their arsenal, mix, and match metrics. The page shows induvidual arsenal metrics of each pitch as well as the overall arsenal metrics. It also shows the mix, match and arsenal scores for each pitch pairing in the arsenal. The graphs at the bottom show a 2D pitch movement plot on the right and the 3D interactive pitch trajectory plot on the left.

Arsenal Editor

The Arsenal Editor page allows you to create your own arsenal and see how the mix, match and arsenal metrics change in real time. You can add, remove, and edit pitches to your liking. The editor page will have the same elements as the Arsenal Explorer page.
